## Starting the App

After installation, launch Finzytrack and you'll be greeted with the main dashboard.

## Your First Import

1. Navigate to the **Import** view from the sidebar
2. Upload a bank statement (OFX or CSV)
3. Review and confirm the imported transactions

## Exploring the Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of your finances through customizable widgets. See [Dashboards](/views/dashboards/) for a tour of the dashboard view, or [Dashboard Recipes](/reference/dashboard-recipes/) for details on customization.
